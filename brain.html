Brain clay

Instructions

In this exercise, you will interact with a 3D model of the brain. The goal is to drag every object in the correct place on the Ventricle model. Once you have placed an object in the correct position in the correct rotation, it will snap to that position and you won't be able to move it anymore. If you want to zoom in on the 3D model, click the "Zoom" button. This will enlarge the 3D model to fill the screen, allowing you to see it in more detail. If you get stuck you can consult the final result image below to see how the objects should be placed.

Controls

  • Click and drag the objects to move them.
  • Use the Left and Right arrow key to rotate the objects either clockwise or counterclockwise respectively.
  • Use the scrollwheel to zoom in or out.
  • Use the right mouse button to pan the view.
  • Use shift + drag to move the view.
