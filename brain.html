Brain clay

Instructions

In this exercise, you will interact with a 3D model of the human brain. The goal is to drag every neuroanatomical object in the correct location around the ventricular system. Once you have placed an object in the correct position and in the correct rotation, it will snap to that position and you won't be able to move the object anymore. To see the 3D model in more detail, click the 'Zoom' button. The model will expand to full screen for a closer view. If you need some guidance, consult images of the final stage in three different views below.

Controls

  • Click and drag the objects to move them.
  • Use the Left and Right arrow key to rotate the objects either clockwise or counterclockwise respectively.
  • Use the scrollwheel to zoom in or out.
  • Use the right mouse button to pan the view.
  • Use shift + drag to move the view.
